Customer Growth & Retention Operating Plan

Springs Wellness Massage Studio • Practical, Measured, Sustainable

This document defines how customers are acquired, converted, retained, and measured. Nothing here assumes luck, viral growth, or endless energy. This system is designed to be run every week without burnout.

1. Weekly Task Checklist (PDF-Ready)

TaskFrequencyOutcome
Respond to all WhatsApp enquiries within 10 minutesDailyHigher booking conversion
Confirm next-day bookings & rulesDailyReduced no-shows
Post in 2 local community groupsWeeklyNew enquiries
Follow up past clients (30–45 days)WeeklyRepeat bookings
Review calendar gapsWeeklyFill unused slots
Review KPIs (below)WeeklyEarly problem detection
Rule: If a task doesn’t directly increase bookings, retention, or quality, it doesn’t belong in the weekly checklist.

2. WhatsApp Message Templates (Realistic & Boundary-Safe)

First Contact Auto-Reply

“Thank you for contacting Springs Wellness Massage Studio. We are appointment-only. Please let us know the service you’re interested in and your preferred time.”

Booking Confirmation

“Your massage is confirmed for [Day, Time]. Please arrive 5 minutes early. Payment via EFT or cash. We operate under a strict professional code of conduct.”

Post-Session Follow-Up

“Thank you for visiting today. Based on your muscle tension, most clients benefit from another session in 1–2 weeks. Let me know if you’d like me to reserve a spot.”

Reactivation Message (30+ Days)

“Hi, this is Springs Wellness. It’s been a few weeks since your last session — we have an opening this week if you’d like to continue your maintenance.”

5. Client Conversation Scripts That Convert

During Session (Soft Qualification)

“Do you usually wait until pain builds up, or do you prefer regular maintenance?”

Closing the Session

“Your shoulders responded well. Most clients like you come every 2–3 weeks. Would you like me to secure a follow-up slot?”

Handling Price Sensitivity

“I understand. Many clients choose fewer sessions rather than lower quality. Let’s find a rhythm that works for you.”

Never chase clients. Your role is to guide, not convince.
